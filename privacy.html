Padel Papi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

EnglishPortuguêsEspañol

Padel Papi is a booking app that connects padel players with padel coaches. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

What we store

Players (no account required):

  • The name and phone number you type when booking — visible only to the coach you booked with, so they know who is coming and can reach you.
  • Optionally, a note to the coach and your self-declared skill level, shown to the coach (and the level to players in the same group session).
  • An anonymous identifier created by the app, so your bookings belong to your device (or to your Apple ID, if you optionally link one).
  • A push-notification token and your device language, if you allow notifications — used only to send booking confirmations, waitlist updates and lesson reminders, in your language.
  • Payment status only (e.g. "marked as sent", "confirmed by coach"). Padel Papi never processes, holds, or sees actual payments — those happen directly between you and the coach (MB WAY or cash).
  • Attendance status (e.g. a no-show marked by the coach) as part of the coach's records.

Coaches:

  • Your account email, and your public page (name, club, address, bio, prices, availability, MB WAY number if you enable it).

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no analytics profiles, no selling or sharing of data with third parties.
  • No payment processing — money never flows through the platform.
  • No tracking across other apps or websites.

Where data lives

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Messaging) in the region chosen when the project was created. Firebase's own terms and security measures apply.

Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access & portability: the app shows you everything it holds about you (your lessons, your details on each booking).
  • Erasure: in the app, open My Lessons → ⋯ → Delete my data. This cancels your upcoming bookings, removes you from every waitlist, and deletes your notification token. Your name on past sessions remains in the coach's records (legitimate interest: their attendance and payment history), and is not linked to an active identity after deletion.
  • Rectification: your name and phone are re-entered on each booking — simply book with corrected details, or ask the coach to remove an incorrect entry.

Children

The app is intended for users aged 13 and over. Bookings for younger players should be made by a parent or guardian.
